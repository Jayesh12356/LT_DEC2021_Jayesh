Peaceful Mountains

The Naksan Park, is located in the old downtown of the city, along the Seoul city wall. This park is a unique hot spot to enjoy the Seoul nightscape. You can reach the Naksan park either from the Dongdeumun area or the Daehakro area. Both ways offer diffrent, but very unique and enjoyable experiences. The Naksan Park is a famous filming set of many korean dramas. For this reason, it has become a hot spot for dating among couples, and an attractive touristic area for visitors. Also, it is possible to reach the famous Ihwa mural village by walking down the wall. The village is a very instagrammable area with cute cafes, and art murals. Another great acitivity , will be taking night photography of the city. I especially like this place because I can see the whole view of Seoul. You can see a mixture of skyscrappers, the city gate, and the busy city streets. It is the perfect spot to capture the dynamics of Seoul, and reproduce the nightscape with your lense.

Trekking Adventures

The vast wilderness of the Himalayan state of Uttarakhand is an ideal place for adventures and nature lovers to explore and experience. Himalaya trekking and mountaineering in Uttarakhand is quite popular with numerous treks and expeditions taking place in the higher altitudes. Both Summer and Winter treks are equally popular in Uttarakhand. Long chain of mountains and inviting huge groups of adventure seekers year after year, Uttarakhand is a true heaven for trekking. Plenty of activities like trekking, hiking, climbing, mountaineering are on fingertips to thrill breaths. Lofty peaks, salubrious climate, lush greeneries add more enthusiasm to do. Professional and local Uttarakhand trek guides are also available and it is advised to try all adventure sports within their knowledge.

Beautiful Lakes

In the southern tip of South America is one of the landscapes that seem to be taken from a fairy tale. Pehoé Lake is located in the Torres del Paine National Park, a natural wonder in Chilean Patagonia. Getting to this place is a bit difficult, but the view you get makes everything worthwhile. This Antarctic region full of mountains with snowy peaks is one of the must-see landscapes that you can find in Chile. Due to the majesty of the scenery, Lake Pehoé is one of the most beautiful lakes in the world.

Sunset Beaches

The Indian coastline is one of the longest in the world with incredibly scenic beaches. And lately the best beaches in India, especially the ones on the west coast are trending in the travel scene. The beaches here offer pulsating adventure to serene solitude, and of course lively parties. Each beaches in India have something different to offer and altogether a different experience. From vibrant parties to water sports, spending time in leisure to private coves, there is something in store for all kinds of travelers.
